# Setting Up a Custom Domain

## Option 1: Update Project Name in Vercel

1. Go to [Vercel Dashboard](https://vercel.com/dashboard)
2. Click on your `personal-website` project
3. Go to **Settings** → **General**
4. Change the **Project Name** to something cool like:
   - `jaswanth-dev`
   - `jaswanth-portfolio`
   - `jaswanth-profile`
   - `jaswanth-io`

5. Your new URL will be: `https://your-project-name.vercel.app`

## Option 2: Add a Custom Domain

1. Go to [Vercel Dashboard](https://vercel.com/dashboard)
2. Click on your `personal-website` project
3. Go to **Settings** → **Domains**
4. Click **Add Domain**
5. Enter your custom domain (e.g., `jaswanth.dev`, `jaswanth.io`)
6. Follow the DNS configuration instructions
